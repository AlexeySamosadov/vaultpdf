How

  1. Open the VaultPDF app, drop in your PDF (or several).
  2. Drag the page thumbnails into the order you want.
  3. Click Export PDF — the reordered file saves locally.

Why local

  • No upload — your document stays on your machine.
  • Combine + reorder pages from multiple PDFs at once.
  • Works offline, no account, no tracking.
