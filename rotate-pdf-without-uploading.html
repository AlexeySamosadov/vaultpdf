Rotate a PDF — without uploading it

Scanned a document sideways or upside down? Fix the orientation and save it permanently — without sending the file to a website. VaultPDF rotates PDF pages entirely in your browser and bakes the rotation into the saved file.

How to rotate PDF pages privately

  1. Open the VaultPDF app and drop in your PDF.
  2. Click the pages you want to rotate (or All).
  3. Hit Rotate until they’re upright — each tap turns them 90°.
  4. Click Export PDF — the rotation is saved into the new file, locally.

Why local

  • No upload — the document never leaves your machine.
  • Permanent — the rotation is written into the exported PDF, not just a view setting.
  • No account, works offline.
