How to convert a PDF to images privately

  1. Open the VaultPDF app and drop in your PDF.
  2. Optionally select just the pages you want.
  3. Click PNG (.zip) — every page is rendered locally and saved as a zip of images.

Why do it in the browser

  • No upload — confidential pages aren’t exposed to any server.
  • Crisp output — pages render at 2× for sharp images.
  • No account, no tracking, works offline.
