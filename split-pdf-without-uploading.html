How to split a PDF privately

  1. Open the VaultPDF app.
  2. Drop in your PDF — every page appears as a thumbnail.
  3. Click to select exactly the pages you want to keep.
  4. Click Export PDF — only your selected pages are saved, locally.

Tip: select nothing and Export to save the whole document; select a range to split out just those pages.

Why local splitting is safer

  • No upload — the original never leaves your computer.
  • No leftovers on a server — there’s no server, so there’s nothing to retain or breach.
  • Works offline — disconnect and it still splits.
