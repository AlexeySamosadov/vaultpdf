Delete pages from a PDF — without uploading it

Need to drop a page or two from a PDF — a cover sheet, a blank, a confidential page — without sending the whole document to a random website? VaultPDF removes pages entirely in your browser. The file never leaves your device.

Open VaultPDF — delete pages →

How

  1. Open the VaultPDF app and drop in your PDF.
  2. Every page shows as a thumbnail — click the ones to remove.
  3. Hit Delete, then Export PDF — the trimmed file saves locally.

Why local

  • No upload — confidential pages aren't exposed to any server.
  • Works offline — proof nothing is sent.
  • No account, no tracking.
